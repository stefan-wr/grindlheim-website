Welcome to the theme world of Grindlheim

Grindlheim is an highly detailed and immersive theme world being created in Planet Coaster. Inspired by Klugheim at Phantasialand, Grindlheim features two rollercoasters: K'aros, an intense and fast multi launch coaster with quick turn-arounds, frightening head-choppers and an entwined layout and Ri'kh, a family coaster which takes you on a short journey through the heart of Grindlheim.
